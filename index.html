We look forward to welcoming you to Oitizzo soon. There are few easy steps to orders or reservations at Oitizzo. Follow the steps one by one, then you will successfully make an order or reservation. Here we go...

  1. Take a look of whole page and find out reservation button.Then Click it. It will take you to our menu page.

  2. At menu page you will find our food items which are available on times. At every food item you will find cart button. Then find out a cart button which is located at the corner of navigation bar or top right corner. Click the cart button.

  3. After clicking cart button the page open up an order form. The form have 2 section one for order section another for reservation section. In the form you will see many form fields such as your item name input field, location field, date and time field etc.

  4. Filled out all the field correctly and click submit button. You will receive a confirmation email once your order or reservation has been successful. If you do not receive this confirmation email, there may have been an error in your order or reservation.

    5. Note : Please view our reservation and cancellation policy ahead of your visit to Oitizzo.
